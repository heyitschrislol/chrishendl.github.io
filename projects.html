Program Description:

WHY:

The original intention behind writing this program was to help my girlfriend with her accounting homework. I thought I could use what I was learning in class to make something that would alleviate some of the repetitiveness of her accounting homework calculations. Truth be told, a large part of my motivation came from excitement at what I was learning in class, and I was rather anxious to see what my newfound "knowledge" could do.
